Clothing & Accessories



Upgrades That Make a Difference

3 Layer Outer Shell
As an advanced skier, you are out in harsher conditions and sweating more. Upgrading to a 3 layer shell provides maximum waterproofing while remaining highly breathable.
REI Evo


Packable Down Jacket
Fleece is great for starters, but a high fill down jacket packs down incredibly small in your backpack and offers superior warmth for freezing summit winds.
Amazon REI


Leather Freeride Gloves
Ditch the cheap synthetic gloves. High quality treated leather gloves offer unmatched durability against ski edges and better dexterity for adjusting gear on the fly.
Amazon Evo


Magnetic Goggles
Lighting conditions change fast on big mountains. Magnetic lens systems allow you to swap from a dark sun lens to a low light lens in seconds without taking your helmet off.
Amazon REI




Equipment & Safety



Buying the Right Hardware

High Performance Skis
Advanced skiers need stiffer skis with metal layers for stability at high speeds. Look for a wider waist for powder or a stiffer all mountain ski for hardpack snow.
Evo Skis.com


Stiff Flex Ski Boots
Renting is over. You need boots that transfer your energy instantly to your skis. Advanced boots have a stiffer flex, giving you total precision and control on steep terrain.
Evo


Avalanche Safety Gear
If you are venturing into the backcountry, this is non negotiable. You must carry a modern transceiver, a collapsible metal shovel, and a probe, and you must know how to use them.
REI Evo


🛠️ Custom Boot Fitting

As an advanced skier, the best money you will ever spend is on a professional boot fitting. Getting custom footbeds and having the plastic shell punched to fit your exact foot shape will completely change your skiing experience.

Explore some of the most famous and challenging Ski resorts from around the world.

Whistler Blackcomb, Canada




Whistler Blackcomb is one of the most famous ski resorts in North America. It offers a massive variety of expert terrain, steep alpine bowls, and world class off piste skiing. Advanced riders will love the sheer scale of the mountains, delivering deep powder and endless black diamond runs. It is the perfect place to challenge your skills and experience big mountain riding at its absolute best.

Zermatt, Switzerland




Zermatt is an iconic destination instantly recognizable by the towering Matterhorn. It is home to some of the highest skiing in Europe, ensuring incredible snow quality all season long. Advanced skiers can tackle massive vertical drops, challenging glacial terrain, and endless freeride routes. The resort combines luxury with extreme high altitude thrills, making it a bucket list spot for expert level riders.

St. Anton, Austria




St. Anton is legendary in the ski world, famous for its incredibly challenging slopes and intense off piste routes. It is the birthplace of modern alpine skiing and continues to be a top tier destination for experts seeking deep powder and steep, technical runs. After pushing your limits on the mountain all day, you can experience the world famous apres ski scene in the vibrant village below.
